The easiest way to tell a story.

Talkalog is the easiest way to capture stories, destination memos, and audio recordings on-the-go. As simple as a phone call and easier than writing by hand.

Audio Heirlooms

My grandparents are first generation Filipino-Americans and my childhood was filled with stories like "When I was a little girl..." or "When I arrived in the US, I had three dollars in my pocket...".

Inspired by my desire to preserve my family's oral history before it's too late, Talkalog started as a way to be able to easily capture these stories, no matter how far apart we are or technologically challenged they may be.

On the Web, Over the Phone

With multiple ways to capture these audio recordings, each of these files become instant heirlooms: a way for future generations to understand where they came from, and the possibilities are endless.

Send requests to capture oral histories from family, or use it to create audio blogs on the web or with a simple phone call. Transcript generation and rich text formatting allows these stories to be starting points for beautiful blog posts, recipe books, emails, scripts, novels, and more.
